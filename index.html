About Me

who I am

I first started coding my senior year of high school and realized how much functionality and capability you can build just by writing out a few lines of code. I am now a full stack junior web developer from San Francisco who loves tinkering with code and building apps. Most of my workflow includes the use of Javascript, AngularJS, NodeJS, Bootstrap, and Test-Driven Development in Jasmine.


Throughout my life, I’ve always been passionate about helping others and giving back to the community as much as I can. I care a lot about preserving one’s culture and roots as I think it’s very important. I was grateful enough to have had the opportunity to help the younger generation of my people learn about our culture and traditions. I was very involved in assisting the students and teachers.


I’m excited to see many opportunities open up for me as I’m building my career path as a fullstack developer. I look forward to meeting a community who enjoys helping others and coding.
