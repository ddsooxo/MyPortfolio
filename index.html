About Me

who I am

I'm a front end developer and most of my work flow includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MVC (Model View Controller), Rest APIs, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I also have experience in back end development. I can build applications/products from back end development to front end development. I also follow the principles of Test Driven Development (TDD) which is crucial for many developers. Although writing tests was a struggle for me, I definitely think it was worth the battle to write them because they test the functionality of your code and will save you from getting errors. It ensures that the end results of your product are well rounded and fully functional.

I'm currently interested in internships in the Bay Area/San Francisco as I want to continue to develop my career here.
